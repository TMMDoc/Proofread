Organizational measures to be taken by the user

Organizational measures to be taken by the user

##1.1 Adhere to the duty of care when handling the machineOperating the machine with robot In the area of application of the EC machinery directive, the owner or integrator must adhere to the requirements of the EC machinery directive. Integration requires the following documents: risk assessment, EU declaration of conformity and operator’s manual. The specific national requirements for users or integrators must be applied outside the area of applicability of the EC Machinery Directive. The control and the electrical installation of the robot are the responsibility of the user or the integrator. Wiring of the safety-related signals may only be carried out by qualified personnel. The user must secure the machine and the robot in such a way that nobody can be in the danger zone during operation (for example, with the use of safety fencing). Observe the national safety regulations.
