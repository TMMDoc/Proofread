Sensor backgauge fingers

Following consultation with TRUMPF, integrators may also install third party sensor backgauge fingers. The integrator is thereby responsible for ensuring that the appropriate finger geometries and dimensions are set and selected in the press control system. The dynamics of the backgauge axes, in particular the Z1 and Z2 axes, must be reduced if the dimensions of the special sensor backgauge finger are significantly greater than the dimensions of the standard backgauge finger. If this is not done, the backgauge axes controllers will notify an overload and switch off. The robot interface signal courses are not dependent on the sensor backgauge fingers and therefore remain the same.
