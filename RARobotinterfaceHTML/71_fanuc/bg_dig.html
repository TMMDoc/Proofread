Digital backgauge sensor

  • The digital backgauge sensor is designed to detect when the sheet metal makes physical contact with the backgauge.

  • Each backgauge is equipped with an independent touch-based sensor.

  • When the sheet touches the sensor, it immediately activates the corresponding Digital Input (DI) assigned to that backgauge.

  • If there is no contact, the DI remains OFF.

  • Each backgauge operates separately, allowing the system to identify which side has been touched.
