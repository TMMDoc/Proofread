The robot interface is designed for operating a press brake with Right Angle control along with any industrial robot. It is also possible to operate the TruBend 1000 Series in the normal manner using the control panel via manual feed, while complying with the safety regulations, the corresponding national standards and legislation and the additional safety information in this description.

An industrial robot can be connected to the TruBend via the interface provided for this purpose. Together, they form a work cell.

During the connection of an industrial robot, the operator must adhere to the valid safety and accident prevention regulations of the respective country and the safety laws of the state and the region.

The press beam moves at rapid speed and the robot moves.
Serious injury or death!

The user must secure the machine and the robot in such a way that nobody can be in the danger zone during operation (for example, with the use of safety fencing). Observe country-specific safety regulations.

The robot interface consists of a 42-pin plug for the cable connection with the robot, a Right Angle module with digital inputs and outputs for the signals from the control, a switch panel with relay and an additional key switch with two positions at the left side of the electrical cabinet.
