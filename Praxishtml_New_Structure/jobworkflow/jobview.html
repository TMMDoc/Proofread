Job view

Job view

Selecting the jobs option displays a list of all jobs currently set up in the software. You can double-click on a job to view its details or right-click to access additional options.

  • Jobs are displayed in tiles view, where each job is displayed with basic properties like Job ID, Job Ref, Customer name, Total parts, Due Date, Job status bar etc.

  • Each job tile also displays part images in a stacked card format. You can scroll through all parts in a job using the mouse wheel.

Job details

  • Double-clicking a tile or pressing the Enter key after selecting a tile switches the view to Detail Mode.

  • The details page displays the parts list along with the nested sheets (layouts), if any.

  • Use the Up/Down arrow keys to switch between jobs in the details page.

  • Scrolling the mouse wheel over the part stack highlights the part in both the part list and the layouts.

  • Selecting a layout highlights the layout and all parts nested on it in the part list. The highlights automatically turn off after a minute.

  • Nest summary:

    • Parts: Displays the total number of order items and the total quantity in the job.

    • Sheets: Displays the total number of nested sheets along with the total sheet quantity.

  • Work Steps:

    • Bending: Shows the machine assigned and its order quantity to be processed.

    • Cutting: Shows the machine assigned and its order quantity to be processed.

Additional information

There is also three additional icons on the right of the screen which are Simulate, Goto and Back.

  • Simulate will open the selected item for a more detailed view.

  • Goto will switch the focus and display on the part or the nest that is selected.

  • Back will go back one level from where you currently are, for example clicking Back while in simulation will take you back to the main job and from a job will take you back to the main jobs display.
