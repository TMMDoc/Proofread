Job status

  • The Job status bar uses color codes to indicate the progress or condition of the job based on the quantity processed or remaining.

  • The parts within the job and the nest sheets are also highlighted with matching colors to reflect their individual status within the overall JOB.

  • Color coding improves tracking and ensures that operators can easily see the production status at a glance.
