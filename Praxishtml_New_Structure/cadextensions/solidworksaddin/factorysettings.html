Factory settings

Factory settings

This setting allows users to view and update both Praxis factory configurations and Flux settings directly from SolidWorks. This includes parameters such as Flux materials, bend catalogs, custom bend deductions, and other machine-related configurations that influence part feasibility and production readiness.

The feature provides a convenient way to review or adjust manufacturing settings without leaving the CAD environment—especially when performing feasibility checks or design corrections that depend on factory-specific configurations.

Accessing the Factory Settings from within SolidWorks is especially useful when:

  • A feasibility check fails due to outdated or incorrect material/bend settings.

  • Design engineers need to verify bend radii, allowances, or machine limits without switching between multiple applications.

  • Collaboration between the CAD and CAM teams requires quick cross-verification of production parameters.

Administrative permissions are required to modify or save machine or factory settings.

Users without admin rights can still view all configurations for reference.
